Codes are used to organize the LBA-ECO investigations by science theme:

CD - Carbon Dynamics
LC - Land Cover and Land Use Change
ND - Nutrient Dynamics and Surface Water Chemistry
PC - Physical Climate
TG - Trace Gas and Aerosol Fluxes
