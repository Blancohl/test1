TITLE: Nitrogen in surface water and precipitation at Santarem Region

Many workers sre studying nitrogen whole the word. In Amazon region few studies are made, specially after intensive agriculture use of the soils near the forest haven't been proposed before. Our results show that agriculture release to the atmosphere and to surface water high quantity of the nitrogen (mineral and organic).
