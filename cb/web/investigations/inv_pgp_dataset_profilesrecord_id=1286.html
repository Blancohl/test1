Dataset Abstract:

 We used Landsat data from 1984 (MSS or multispectral scanner), 1994 (TM5 or thematic mapper 5), 1995 (TM5) and 1999 (TM7) and field work to examine the land use, land cover, and land cover change dynamics in the county of Sao Francisco do Para located in the Bragantina region, the oldest agriculture frontier in Amazonia. These data are only for the municipio (county) and immediate region. The region is east of the major city of Belem.

Data Characteristics (Entity and Attribute Overview):

 Data Characteristics:

All Landsat data are subsets of full imagery from Landsat path row 223/61, radiometrically and geometrically corrected. Both raw DN (8 Bit) and reflectivity (float) data sets are available. MSS data are 62 meter resolution. TM data are 30 meters. All data are UTM projected, datum is WGS84. All data are ERDAS Imagine *.img files

Data Application and Derivation:

Supervised classification of the 1999 imagery showed that forests of differing ages classes occupied 22%, 13%, 9%, and 6% of the area, young to old, respectively.

Quality Assessment (Data Quality Attribute Accuracy Report):

 Quality Assessment:

Kappa statistic and field evaluations were used with the 1999 classification to determine classification accuracies.

Process Description:

 Data Acquisition Materials and Methods:

All satellite data were acquired through USGS EROS or through the Brazilian Space Agency, INPE.
