Search Tips

Search Fields: Alone or in any combination.
FirstName    
LastName   
Email    
Organization   
Project Group

TEXT fields
To enter multiple words, separate by comma with no space or quotes. 
Partial words, word combinations and e-mail addresses can also be searched.

Multiple Selections
To select multiple items from one pick list, hold down Ctrl while clicking.

EXAMPLES:

Search:  LastName: Keller,Tokay (no spaces).
Result: everyone that has “Keller” or “Tokay” in the LastName field.

Search: Carbon Dynamics (CD) science groups.
Multi-select all groups with Carbon Dynamic code "CD" using Ctrl key.
Result: all members of Carbon Dynamics (CD) science groups.

Search: Press search button without any selections from the search fields.
Result: all members of the Personnel database.

For additional help or specific search requests, please contact the Project Account Administrator for assistance.
