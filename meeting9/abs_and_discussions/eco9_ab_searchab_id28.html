Scenarios for econometrics projections: The future of the Amazon

Projecting future of Amazon land cover in the interesting of understands its sustainability properties; require both modeling and the developing of likely scenarios. Our project entitled A Basic Scale Econometric Model for Projecting Future Amazon Landscape is developing both modeling approach and scenarios upon which scenarios would be base. This poster presents elements of the scenarios that will be used in our efforts to understand the future of Amazon. We present here in this poster these scenarios along three dimensions, (1) demographic trends; (2) infrastructure developments, and (3) protected areas and zoning regulations. For demographic trends, the poster presents regional population projections, for both rural and urban areas, under various assumptions about fertility, mortality, and migration. It applies standard demographic techniques, and discusses magnitudes of change relative to current levels. Regarding infrastructure development, the poster pays special attention to Avança Brasil. Specifically, we present (1) the plan in it full detail as first proposed by President Cardoso, and (2) the plan as it is likely to be implemented in the short to mid-run, given priorities of the current administration. As for protected areas and zoning regulations, we present graphics showing the current scheme of protected areas, including conversation and preservation units of various types; we discuss pertinent environmental regulations in this regard. We also present graphic on planned land use zones in Pará, Mato Grosso, and Rondonia. Finally, the poster contains text describing how the scenario information is utilized by econometric modeling to project future landscapes.
